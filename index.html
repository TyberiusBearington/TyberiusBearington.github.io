Why attend Ellucian Live 2025?

In less than six months, Ellucian will bring The Magic of Innovation to Orlando, Florida as we convene our global community of higher education leaders in the Sunshine State for Ellucian Live 2025!

Early Bird Registration is now open! Secure your best tickets rates by December 13.

But why should you or your institution attend higher education's premier technology conference? Let's explore our 2025 key themes and discover why Ellucian Live will be the must-attend higher education event of the year.

Imagine.

Imagine the possibilities of a magical location!

What greater location to ignite imagination and creativity than Orlando, Florida?! Home to magical attractions, childlike wonder, and plenty of sunshine, we are thrilled to bring our global community to this center of inspiration as we embrace the magic of connections and innovation.

Ellucian Live and its partner hotels are located on Orlando's famed International Drive, where restaurants, rides, and resorts abound! With prime spring weather and theme parks like Disney World and Universal Studios in Ellucian Live's backyard, there will be plenty of opportunities to explore the beautiful Orlando area.

Believe.

Believe in the power of the global higher education community.

Last year, Ellucian Live brought together nearly 6,000 higher education professionals, technology leaders, Ellucian employees, and partners under one roof - and 2025 aims to gather a larger community than ever before! This is a prime chance to learn more about the power of Ellucian products, expand your partnerships, and connect with peers from around the world .

All customers receive complimentary access to our renowned Customer Appreciation Event, where Ellucian Live will undoubtedly bring the party to several local restaurants and attractions. It is guaranteed to be a spectacular time!

Achieve.

Achieve greatness for students around the world through the transformative power of technology.

Of course, the heart of Ellucian Live is the content we bring you from higher education's top technology leaders. From inspiring keynotes, product reveals and updates, and celebrity guests, our Main Stage will be the core of the electrifying atmosphere of Ellucian Live.

After Main Stage, each day promises hundreds of sessions delivering key insights to attendees. With nine different tracks and a customizable schedule builder, you can design an experience tailored to your exact needs and desires regardless of your field or title. This is your opportunity to get all your Ellucian updates in one place, along with sessions focused the hottest topics in higher education.

Students are at the heart of Ellucian's mission and vision, and the common thread of all Ellucian Live content is empowering student success globally. As the singular technology provider focusing solely on higher education, Ellucian Live is guaranteed to enhance your institution's technological capabilities and address your needs as a higher education professional.

Register Now! We can't wait to see you April 6-9 at Ellucian Live 2025!

    Ellucian

    Ellucian powers innovation for higher education, partnering with more than 2,900 customers across 50 countries, serving 20 million students. Fueled by decades of experience with a singular focus on the unique needs of learning institutions, the Ellucian platform features best-in-class SaaS capabilities and delivers insights needed now and into the future. These solutions and services span the entire student lifecycle, including data-rich tools for student recruitment, enrollment, and retention to workforce analytics, fundraising, and alumni engagement.

    Ellucian's innovative solutions, vast ecosystem of partners and user community of more than 45,000 provides best practices leading to greater institutional success and achieving better student outcomes.
